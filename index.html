Fairytale Matching Game

  • The story characters have been hidden.
  • Every character is placed two times in the coffers.
  • You can select only two coffers every turn.
  • Your mission is to find the pair of characters in the least number of tries.
  • When a pair is found, both will remain face up, otherwise, will be turned down.
  • Less number of tries are better scores!
